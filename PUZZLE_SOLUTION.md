# How to Solve the Ζωή Puzzle

## Step 1: Enter the Site
Click the **"Συνέχεια ♥"** button on the welcome page.

## Step 2: Decode the Hidden Message
The page looks like an academic paper, but the mathematical equations hide a secret message.

### Clue 1: The Convergence Theorem
Look at the index mapping:
> λ → ε → ι → π

These Greek letters spell **λείπ** (from "λείπεις" meaning "I miss you").

### Clue 2: The Boundary Conditions
Look at the boundary conditions:
> θ₀ = ε₁ = λ₂ = ω₃

These Greek letters spell **θέλω** (meaning "I want you").

## Step 3: Enter the Answer
Type one of these in the input field:
- **miss** or **want** (English)
- **λείπεις** or **θέλω** (Greek)

## Step 4: Open the Envelope
Click the wax-sealed envelope that appears to reveal the love letter.

---

## The Message
After solving, a beautiful animated envelope opens revealing a love letter for Ζωή from Νίκος:

> *"Μου λείπεις τόσο πολύ... Σε θέλω κοντά μου"*
>
> *"I miss you so much... I want you near me"*
